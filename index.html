Installing

1. With pathogen

Checkout of the project:

$ git clone git://github.com/gilsondev/lizard.git bundles/lizard-colorscheme

1. With vundle

Enter in your bundles:

" Lizard colorscheme
Bundle 'gilsondev/lizard'
